    Purpose

    The purpose of our project is to explore the connections between the wealth and development of different countries in connection with the quality of their education. Through our project, we hope to bring attention toward the discrepancies in the quality of education on a global scope.

    Data Visualizations

    This visualization displays the developmental level classification that UNICEF has assigned to each country. The countries represented are only the countries that UNICEF has education data for, and therefore are the only countries that will be analyzed in this project.
